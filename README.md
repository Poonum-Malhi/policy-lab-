#  Policy Lab

> **A flight simulator for economics** — Learn, experiment, and understand the complex trade-offs that policymakers face.

---

##  What is Policy Lab?

Economic policy shapes our world — from AI regulation to carbon taxes. But most of us only see the results after laws are passed.

**Policy Lab** lets you:
-  **Rewrite real OECD/IMF policy proposals**
-  **Adjust variables** (tax rates, compliance thresholds, subsidies)
-  **Simulate impacts** across Business, Finance, Technology, and Sustainability
- **See instant AI-generated estimates** of trade-offs

Think of it as a **risk-free space to learn, experiment, and understand policy decisions** that affect the real world.

##  How It Works

1. **Choose a Sector** — Explore policies in Technology, Business, Finance, or Sustainability
2. **Pick a Policy** — Start with real frameworks from OECD, IMF, or World Bank
3. **Tweak the Levers** — Adjust parameters using intuitive sliders
4. **Run the Simulation** — Our AI engine generates estimated impacts
5. **Compare & Learn** — See how your choices compare with community averages and expert recommendations
6. **Export Your Scenario** — Save as Markdown or share results
